Mission

Traditional methods of content design and development are not sufficient to meet the exploding demand for high quality learning experiences in educational institutions and corporates.

Continual Engine is dedicated to finding simpler and less expensive ways to deliver learning at the pace and at a cost that enables scalable solutions.

We use automation, deep learning, machine learning, computer vision and natural language processing techniques to make the process of content discovery, extraction, curation and consumption more precise and efficient.

Our Product Suite

Continual Engine's suite of AI enabled products and our flagship platform is created to support educational institutions, organizations, and learners. We are making learning more accessible, engaging, and smarter for all by applying sophisticated AI technology and innovative solutions. Click on the product icons to know more about them.

Deep Learning and Computer Vision Engineer



    Job description:
  • The engineer will be responsible for design and development of modules that will classify and process images.
  • Develop applications (object detection, classification, localization and analysis, specialized OCR) using deep learning and machine learning techniques.
  • Design scalable and fast web applications.
  • Work with client teams to iterate upon and customize the applications.

    Requirement:
  • Experience in the field of Machine Learning, Computer Vision & Deep Learning.
  • Experience working in Computer Vision libraries, OCR solutions, object detection algorithms
  • Experience working in PDF manipulation libraries is a bonus
  • Strong mathematical understanding of image processing algorithms.

    Skills Required:
  • Deep learning Algorithms – CNN, RNN, LSTM.
  • Object detection algorithm – YOLO, SSD etc.
  • Stack – Python, Django, Mongo DB
  • OCR packages - Tesseract
  • Knowledge of AWS, Javascript & JSON is highly Desirable



Senior Backend Engineer



    Job description:
  • Back-End Development, Python, Django, REST APIs, SQL and Relational Databases (MySQL), AWS/Continuous Integration.
  • Test Driven Development (TDD), JS MVC (React.js/Angular.js).
  • Knowledge of Javascript is a plus.

    Requirement:
  • 3-5 years of professional software development experience.
  • Strong coding skills. Professional experience in developing productionquality, scalable & robust software in Python.
  • Solid understanding of web technologies; HTTP, REST etc.
  • Prior knowledge or experience of working with PDF related technologies is a plus.
  • Excellent knowledge of Relational Databases like MySQL.
  • Experience with AWS or similar cloud computing environments.
